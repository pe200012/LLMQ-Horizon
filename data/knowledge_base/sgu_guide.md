# Japanese University SGU/IGP & Exam Guide

## 1. Program Types
*   **SGU/IGP (English Track)**
    *   **Description**: "Super Global University" or "International Graduate Program". Fully English-taught degree programs.
    *   **Requirements**: No Japanese required. Document screening + Online Interview.
    *   **Key Docs**: TOEFL/IELTS, GRE (optional for some), Research Proposal (CRITICAL).
    *   **Timeline**: Apply 9-12 months before entry.
*   **General Exam (修考 - Regular Master's)**
    *   **Description**: Traditional Japanese entrance exam.
    *   **Requirements**: High Japanese (N1 level usually), Written Exam (Math, Physics, Major subject) on campus.
    *   **Timeline**: Exam in Aug/Feb.
*   **Research Student (研究生)**
    *   **Description**: Non-degree pre-master student. "Foot in the door".
    *   **Purpose**: Prepare for the General Exam (修考) under a professor's supervision.

## 2. Major SGU Programs (Top 5)
*   **University of Tokyo (UTokyo)**: IMEP (Eng), UTIPE (Econ), GSP (Sci).
*   **Kyoto University**: International Course in Civil Engineering, East Asia Course (Econ).
*   **Science Tokyo (Tokyo Tech)**: IGP-A (MEXT Scholarship), IGP-C (General). *Note: Renamed to Institute of Science Tokyo*.
*   **Waseda University**: FSE (Sci & Eng), WBS (Business), GSICCS.
*   **Keio University**: IGP (Sci & Tech), GIGA (SFC).

## 3. Standard Timeline (Fall/September Entry)
*   **Oct - Dec (Year -1)**: Take TOEFL/GRE. Research Labs.
*   **Jan - Mar (Year 0)**: **Contact Professors** (Crucial for IGP-A/B). Send CV + Research Proposal.
*   **Apr - May (Year 0)**: Official Application Submission.
*   **Jun - Jul (Year 0)**: Interviews & Results.
*   **Sep/Oct (Year 0)**: Enrollment.
